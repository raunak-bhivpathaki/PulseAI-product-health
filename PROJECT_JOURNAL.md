# PulseAI Project Journal

## Project

**PulseAI: Product Adoption, Customer Health & Retention Intelligence**

## Fictional Company

**LaunchGrid**

## Central Question

Which customer accounts successfully adopt LaunchGrid, which accounts are losing product momentum, and which behaviors are most strongly associated with retention and churn?

## Project Principles

- Use the public dataset only as raw analytical material.
- Create an original business story and analytical framework.
- Preserve raw data without modification.
- Validate calculations before creating dashboard conclusions.
- Distinguish correlation from causation.
- Make the Customer Health Score transparent and interpretable.
- Document all assumptions and limitations.
- Manually validate AI-assisted recommendations.

# Business Requirements

## Project

PulseAI: Product Adoption, Customer Health & Retention Intelligence

## Fictional Company

LaunchGrid

## Business Problem

LaunchGrid's Product Growth and Customer Success teams currently rely on lagging indicators such as cancellations and non-renewals to identify customer risk.

Leadership needs a proactive analytical framework that is capable of identifying declining product adoption and engagement before customers churn.

## Project Goal

Develop an interpretable customer health framework that combines product usage, feature adoption, subscription history, support interactions, and customer behavior into actionable insights.

## Primary Stakeholders

- Director of Product Growth
- Vice President of Customer Success
- Product Managers
- Revenue Operations
- Executive Leadership

## Key Business Questions

### Executive

- What is our customer base?
- How much recurring revenue is protected or at risk?
- What is our overall customer health?

### Product

- Which features drive customer engagement?
- Which features are underutilized?
- Which customer segments demonstrate the strongest product adoption?

### Customer Success

- Which customers require intervention?
- Which support patterns precede churn?
- Which accounts should Customer Success prioritize?

### Executive Decision Making

- Which customers represent the highest retention opportunity?
- Which behaviors distinguish retained customers from churned customers?
- What actions should improve long-term customer retention?

## Planned Deliverables

- SQL analysis
- Data dictionary
- Customer Health framework
- Product Momentum Index
- Executive Tableau dashboard
- GitHub documentation

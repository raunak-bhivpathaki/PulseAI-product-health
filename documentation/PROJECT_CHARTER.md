# PulseAI Project Charter

## Project Name

**PulseAI: Product Adoption, Customer Health & Retention Intelligence**

## Fictional Company

**LaunchGrid**

LaunchGrid is a B2B collaboration platform that helps product, design, and engineering teams coordinate product development, manage workflows, and collaborate across projects.

## Background

LaunchGrid has experienced customer growth, but its Product Growth and Customer Success teams lack a unified framework for measuring whether accounts are successfully adopting the platform.

Leadership currently relies heavily on lagging outcomes such as cancellations and non-renewals. These outcomes indicate that a customer has already been lost but provide limited opportunity for proactive intervention.

## Business Problem

LaunchGrid needs an analytical solution that identifies:

- Accounts that have successfully activated
- Accounts with broad and sustained product adoption
- Accounts showing declining engagement
- Product behaviors associated with retention and churn
- High-value accounts requiring intervention
- Opportunities for onboarding, education, retention, or expansion

## Project Objective

Develop an interpretable product-health framework that combines product usage, feature adoption, customer support, subscription, and retention data.

The solution should help LaunchGrid identify declining product momentum before churn and prioritize the accounts that require action.

## Primary Stakeholder

**Director of Product Growth**

## Supporting Stakeholders

- Vice President of Customer Success
- Product Managers
- Customer Success Managers
- Revenue Operations
- Account Management
- Executive Leadership

## Scope

The project will analyze available customer, subscription, product-usage, support, and churn data.

Planned outputs include:

- Product activation metrics
- Feature-adoption analysis
- Engagement and retention analysis
- Product Momentum Index
- Customer Health Score
- Churn-driver analysis
- Revenue-at-risk reporting
- Account-prioritization view
- Executive Tableau dashboard
- SQL scripts and supporting documentation

## Out of Scope

The project will not:

- Make any customer-facing decisions on its own
- Claim causal relationships without experimental evidence
- Automatically contact or penalize customers
- Present the health score as a validated commercial model
- Use personally identifiable customer information
- Represent LaunchGrid as a real company

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

1. Produces an interpretable account-level health score.
2. Demonstrates whether lower health scores correspond with higher churn.
3. Identifies measurable adoption and engagement patterns.
4. Prioritizes accounts using both customer risk and revenue exposure.
5. Produces actionable recommendations for Product Growth and Customer Success.
6. Clearly documents assumptions, calculations, limitations, and AI usage.

## Data Source Disclosure

This portfolio project uses a publicly available synthetic SaaS dataset as its raw analytical source.

LaunchGrid, PulseAI, the business problem, stakeholder framework, metrics, health-score methodology, analysis, visual design, and recommendations are original components created specifically for this case study.

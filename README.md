# PulseAI

## Product Adoption, Customer Health & Retention Intelligence

PulseAI is an end-to-end AI-powered product analytics case study developed for LaunchGrid, a fictional B2B collaboration platform used by product, design, and engineering teams.

The project explores how customer activation, feature adoption, product engagement, subscription behavior, and support activity relate to retention and churn.

## Business Problem

LaunchGrid’s Product Growth and Customer Success teams lack a scalable method for successfully distinguishing adopted accounts from customers showing early signs of disengagement.

PulseAI will help stakeholders answer:

- Which accounts successfully activate and adopt the product?
- Which product behaviors are associated with retention?
- Which accounts are losing engagement momentum?
- Which features appear most strongly associated with customer stickiness?
- Which accounts require intervention before renewal?
- How much recurring revenue is exposed to retention risk?

## Planned Solution

PulseAI will combine product usage, subscription, support, and churn data to create:

- Activation and adoption metrics
- Product engagement analysis
- Product Momentum Index
- Customer Health Score
- Retention and churn analysis
- Revenue-at-risk reporting
- Account-prioritization recommendations
- Executive Tableau dashboard
- SQL analysis and project documentation
